CC!DELTARUNE is a fan-game of Toby Fox's DELTARUNE, made by me. It is a re-imaginined version of deltarune chapter 3 based on fanmades and leaks.

About The Project:

Hello

The goal of this project is to give my interpretation of Deltarune Chapter3 by making a fangame.

I've started the development a month ago, I've never gave that much time and energy to a project, everyday I try to make something new for the game.

It's very exhausting, but I feel like this game won't die like my other projects,

I canot cancel it because I've come too far to stop, I've made stuff I never did before only in one month, I made sprite/music/art for the game and I showed it to all my friend, I'm even making the game at school.

For now It's just me making stuff and my friend giving some ideas.

We hope you enjoy our little project! It really means a lot to us.
